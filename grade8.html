What I Did on Summer Vacation

English Writing Assignment 1

This summer was all about swimming and relaxing. I didn’t go anywhere exciting, but I spent a lot of time swimming. It was the best way to cool off and also something that I never got bored with.

Sometimes I would swim for hours, going back and forth or just floating around without thinking about much. Other times, I would just mess around, dive under the water, or just sit at the edge with my feet in. It was quiet and peaceful under the water, and it made the time go by faster. It was like everything just slowed down once you were under the water.

The rest of the time, I was probably drying off, taking a nap, or just hanging around. There wasn’t really a schedule, which made it even better. I could swim whenever I wanted to and stop whenever I got tired. It didn’t feel like I was doing something I had to do, but something that just made the day better.

By the end of the summer, I realized that I spent more time swimming than anything else. It wasn’t an exciting or busy vacation, but it was fun and relaxing. Swimming made the summer enjoyable, and that was enough for me.

What Are My 3 Favorite Foods?

English Writing Assignment 11

Everyone has their own favorite foods that they enjoy consuming. Food is not just something we need to survive. Food is a source of comfort, thrill, and even wonderful memories with the people we love. My three favorite foods are steak, sashimi, and ramen. I love these foods because they taste good, they satisfy me, and they give me different sources of enjoyment.

Steak is one of my favorite foods. I love ribeye steak, preferably rare. Sashimi consists of thin slices of raw fish; I enjoy salmon the most. Ramen, especially chicken ramen, is warm and comforting. These foods bring me joy and satisfaction in different ways.

What Are 3 Places You’d Like to Visit?

English Writing Assignment 12

Traveling is a wonderful way to see the world and learn about different cultures. There are many places I would love to visit, but three stand out the most for me: Japan, Germany, and Bali, Indonesia. Each of these places is unique, with its own culture, history, and natural beauty. Visiting them would be an exciting and unforgettable experience.

Japan for its mix of tradition and technology, Germany for its history and architecture, and Bali for tropical beaches and local culture. Traveling to these countries would allow me to experience different ways of life, try new foods, and see amazing sights.
