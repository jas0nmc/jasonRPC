English Writing Assignment 15

One time, during final exam week, my friend was really stressed about his history exam. He felt as if he was buried under all his schoolwork and was going to end up failing. I offered to help him with his studying, and we could get together after school every day. I went over all the material with him, pinpointing key dates and events, and he found it really helpful. He started getting it, and his confidence grew. Finally, he took his exam, and he was still a little nervous, but he did really well, much better than he had anticipated. He even did better than I did! I was surprised, yes, but I was also proud and happy that I could help him get through his exam.
