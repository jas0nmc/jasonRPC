English Writing Assignment 16

Introduction:

My homeschooling program is completely online, and the learning process is mostly video-based. I don’t have to attend live classes, but I watch the videos and then complete my assignments individually. This type of learning process has its own advantages, but it requires a lot of self-discipline and good time management skills.

Body:

Every day, I log in to my online program and check the videos and assignments I have to complete for the day. The learning process involves watching videos where the teacher explains the concepts step by step, and then I have to complete assignments such as quizzes, worksheets, and writing assignments to show my learning outcomes for the day. Since there are no live classes, I have to complete my learning individually, but sometimes it becomes boring, especially when there are no other students to talk to during the learning process. However, I have learned to manage my time and stay focused, and I set my own goals to complete my assignments on time.

Conclusion:

My homeschooling program, mostly based on videos, has taught me a lot, especially in terms of my own responsibility for my learning process. Even though this type of learning is not the same as the traditional learning process, it has its own advantages, especially in terms of the quiet and comfortable learning environment. Even though this type of learning is sometimes very difficult, it has taught me to be more independent and responsible for my own learning process.
