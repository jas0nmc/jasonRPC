English Writing Assignment 14

Paragraph Writing

The best gift that I can remember that I received on my birthday is a laptop. I remember that I was surprised because I saw that it was a laptop, and I had been wanting one of those for a long time. My dad gave it to me because they wanted me to use it for my school work, research, and writing, and it really helped me a lot. I remember that I felt more responsible and grown-up because it is not just a toy, but a gadget that could help me a lot in the future. Until now, I still remember that it is the best gift that I received on my birthday because it is not only useful, but also important.
