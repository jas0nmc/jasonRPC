English Writing Assignment 2 What Is My Favorite Hobby or Free-Time Activity?

My favorite free-time activity is listening to music. It is an activity that I do on a daily basis without even thinking about it. I listen to music when I am bored, tired, or just trying to pass the time. I put my headphones on, pick something, and let it play.

I enjoy listening to music because it is something that I can do in conjunction with whatever else I am doing. I can listen to it while I am lying down, walking around, or just doing nothing. It does not require me to be paying attention to it. It is just something that plays in the background and makes things seem less empty.

I do not listen to one particular genre of music. It depends on my mood. Some days I want to listen to something more loud. Other days I just want something to play in the background. I do not pay attention to the lyrics or think about it too much. If it sounds good, I will continue to play it.

Listening to music is also a good way to break up the day. If everything else feels repetitive, it is a good way to make the time go by faster. It does not solve any problems or really change anything, but it is a good way to keep things from feeling boring.

Listening to music is my favorite hobby because it is simple and always available to me. I do not have to plan anything or dedicate any time to it. I can start and stop whenever I want. It is simple, but it makes my free time feel better, and that is all that matters to me.
