Describe Your Best Friend and What You Like to Do Together

What I value most about my best friend is the presence they provide me with—company. Being with my best friend is comfortable and natural. There are no awkward silences or the need to constantly talk and make plans. Just being with them makes everything better and makes everything more manageable. This is what being friends with this individual means.

Being with my best friend makes me feel like I can be myself around them. There is no need to explain myself or perform in a certain manner when I am with my best friend. I can sit beside them in silence and still feel connected to them while doing my own things. This silence speaks volumes about how good of friends my best friend and I are and how much value I place on being with this individual.

Being with my best friend also makes me feel better when I am going through a storm of stress and other issues. Being with my best friend makes me feel better when I am stressed out and overwhelmed. I do not need to talk to my best friend or have them explain things to me for me to feel better when I am stressed out and overwhelmed. Just being with my best friend makes me feel better.

The essence of what I value about my best friend is not about the things that they and I do together or the things that they and I have in common. It is about the presence that they have on me and the realness of the friendship that they and I have.
