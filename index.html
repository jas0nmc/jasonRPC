About Me

Hello, my name is Jason. I created this blog originally for a school project, but it has evolved into a dedicated space for me to analyze the intersection of human history and individual achievement.

This is where I document my intellectual growth and share my perspectives on the topics that define our civilization.

Philosophy

Exploration of logic, reality, and human thought.

History

Analysis of civilizations and historical forces.

Economics

Study of markets, incentives, and production.

Ethics

Study of morality, responsibility, and purpose.
