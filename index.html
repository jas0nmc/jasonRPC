About Me

Hello, my name is Jason. I created this blog originally for a school project, but it has evolved into a dedicated space for me to analyze the intersection of human history and individual achievement. This is where I document my intellectual growth and share my perspectives on the topics that define our civilization.

Here are the pillars of my research outside of the classroom:

*

Philosophy

An exploration of logic, reality, and the nature of existence. My writing focuses on the power of the rational mind and the fundamental principles that guide human action.

*

History

Analyzing the past not just as a series of dates, but as a record of human struggle and triumph. I examine how ideas have the power to build—or break—empires.

*

Economics

A study of value, production, and the mechanics of the free market. I look at how economic freedom acts as the ultimate catalyst for innovation and progress.

*

Ethics

Investigating the concept of 'The Good Life.' This section explores the moral responsibility of the individual to live with integrity, purpose, and self-reliance.

"A society that gives up freedom for safety will lose both."
