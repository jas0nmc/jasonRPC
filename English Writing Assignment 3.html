English Writing Assignment 3 What Is My Least Favorite School Subject?

My least favorite school subject is English. Apparently, reading hundreds of pages and writing endless essays is supposed to be fun, but I have not yet reached that level of fun yet. While other subjects can be difficult in a simple way, English is like a never-ending cycle of reading, analyzing, and writing about what we just read.

One of the reasons why English is my least favorite school subject is the reading. There is always another chapter, another book, or another passage that we have to read. While reading is not impossible, it is exhausting when it feels like it never ends. By the time I finish one assignment, there is already another one waiting for me.

The writing assignments make it even more enjoyable. There is always another essay to write, plan, draft, and rewrite. Even when I feel like I have said what I wanted to say, I usually have to add more information or make it "deeper." Sometimes it feels like no answer is ever complete enough.

Another difficult part of English is the analyzing. In English, nothing is ever just a story. There is always symbolism, themes, hidden messages, and deeper meanings that we have to find. Sometimes it feels like we are searching for answers that may or may not even be there.

In conclusion, my least favorite school subject is English because of the reading and writing. While I understand that these are important skills, that does not mean that I have to enjoy the work that goes along with it. While I will continue to do the work, it is definitely not my favorite subject.
